Blob Fish

  • Prey: Mollusks and Crabs that cross its path
  • Behavior: Slow Moving/Peaceful
  • Soft bones, few muscles and lacks a swim bladder
  • Threats: Deep-sea Fishing
  • Habitat: Deep waters off the coasts of Australia and New Zealand
  • Diet: Omnivore
