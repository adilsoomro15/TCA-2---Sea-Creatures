Ribbon Eel

  • Prey: Small Fish and Marine Creatures
  • Behavior: Aggressive
  • Start off black with a yellow dorsal fin. Long Body with high dorsal fins.
  • Threats: Large Fish, Barracudas, and Other Moray Eels
  • Habitat: Warm Lagoons and Coastal Reefs of the Indian and Pacific Oceans
  • Diet: Carnivore
