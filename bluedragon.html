Blue Glaucus/Dragon

  • Prey: Portuguese man o' war and the blue button jelly. They store their prey's stinging cells in their bodies to use against predators.
  • Behavior: Aggressive
  • Can grow up to 1.2 inches long.
  • Threats: Loggerhead Sea Turtles and Climate Change
  • Habitat: In Oceans not by the shore or bottom
  • Diet: Carnivore - Large Venomous Prey
